# **MATURITY TRANSFORMATION ROADMAP: STAGE 0 ‚Üí STAGE 1**
## **Professional Services Transformation for Retirement Firms with Wealth Management**

---

## **EXECUTIVE SUMMARY**

Your organization is embarking on a critical transformation journey. Research shows that organizations stuck at Stage 0 (Ad-Hoc) suffer from chaotic operations, hero-dependent processes, and significantly lower performance. Moving to Stage 1 (Defined/Managed) establishes the **foundational discipline** needed for all future maturity advances.

**Key Insight**: Stage 1 is about moving from **chaos to consistency** ‚Äî establishing common definitions, basic processes, and accountability frameworks. Only 22% of companies have formal Marketing-Sales SLAs in place, meaning most organizations remain in Stage 0.

**Critical Success Factor**: All 5 workstreams must advance together. The recommended sequence prioritizes **foundational capabilities** (CRM and Analytics) before scaling operational workstreams.

---

## **THE 5 WORKSTREAMS**

1. **Lead Best Practices (Lead Management)** - Marketing-to-Sales handoff, lead qualification, and follow-up
2. **Measurement & Reporting (Analytics)** - Data integration, reporting infrastructure, and metrics
3. **Practice Management (Professional Services Operations)** - Delivery operations, resource management, and project execution
4. **Salesforce CRM / User Experience** - System adoption, data quality, and user interface
5. **Lead Creation (Demand Generation)** - Marketing programs, content creation, and campaign execution
